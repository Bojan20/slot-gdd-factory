## Free Spins

| Scatters | Spins |
|---|---|
| 3 | 10 |
| 4 | 15 |
| 5 | 20 |

3+ Scatters anywhere triggers Free Spins. **Scatters may stack** —
multiple scatters per reel are common (the title features stacked scatter
symbols that fill the reel column on landing). Stacked S contribute fully
to the trigger total, so a single reel can carry 2-3 scatters that all
count toward the ladder.

Retrigger is disabled — no retrigger is supported during the round.
